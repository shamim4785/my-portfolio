About Me

I am a Statistical Officer at icddr,b, where I specialize in the design and analysis of large-scale public health research. My work focuses on ensuring the statistical integrity of studies that shape health policy in Bangladesh and beyond.

Research Project

Featured Project: Water Quality in Dhaka Slums

The Challenge: High diarrheal disease rates in urban informal settlements.

My Analysis: Used Stata to perform a Poisson regression on household water storage practices across 500+ samples.

The Impact: Identified that secondary contamination during storage was the primary risk factor, leading to new WASH protocol recommendations.
